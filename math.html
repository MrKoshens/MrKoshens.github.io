Books

Here is a collection of books that have deeply influenced my mathematical journey:

  • "Art of Problem Solving" Series: Comprehensive guides to advanced problem-solving techniques.
  • "Principles of Mathematical Analysis" by Walter Rudin: A classic in real analysis.
  • "Graph Theory" by Reinhard Diestel: An engaging book on graph theory with clear explanations.
