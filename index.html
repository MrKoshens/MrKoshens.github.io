Hi, I'm Madhav Verma

Welcome to my portfolio website!

I am an M.Tech student at the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata, India, majoring in Cryptology and Security. I completed my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from BIT Sindri, Dhanbad, India. My research focuses on Cryptography, with interests in Post-Quantum Cryptography, zero-knowledge proofs, secure multiparty computation, and algorithms. I enjoy solving mathematics and physics Olympiad problems, as well as solving puzzles and competitive programming problems in my spare time. Sometimes, I experiment with my Linux machine, trying to make it both beautiful and productive. Check out my blogs on Linux ricing!
